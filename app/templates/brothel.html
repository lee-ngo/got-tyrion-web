Ah, there's nothing quite like a brothel! The charm of the Northern women is enough to thaw the frost you've been feeling since you've arrived.

The madam of the establishment, ROS, walks out in a flowing robe that leaves very little to the imagination. She claps her hands and a row of young maidens walks into the main room, one more beautiful than the last.

Ros: "Lord Tyrion, welcome! Which of these fine ladies do you choose?"

Do you select ROS or a RANDOM woman?

Ros blushes at the audacity of your choice. "Lord Tyrion, you flatter me. However, I cannot abide. I'm afraid the madam of the house is not for sale."

You: "Everything's for sale, milady. Especially when you're a Lannister."

You throw an additional bag of gold on the table. Ros' eyes widen. "Very well, milord. Please come with me."

You walk with raised anticipation to a private room with Ros. As she undresses in front of you, you feel a swell of excitement and pour more wine.

BOOM! Your brother JAIME storms into the room. Perfect timing as always.

Jaime: "Forgive me, little brother. Pressing matters at hand."

You: "You are certainly not forgiven. What could it be, Ser Jaime?"

Jaime: "You've been asked to join the party heading to The Wall."

You: "And what would I be doing at The Wall?"

Jaime: "Meet with the Night's Watch. Provide a full report to the Small Council upon your return to King's Landing."

You sigh and begin to gather your clothes. Ros looks back at you, confused. "And now my watch begins...."

Ros: "Excellent choice, Lord Tyrion."

A young maiden escorts you to one of their finest rooms. Before you remove your clothes, however, the maiden pulls out a DAGGER and SLITS YOUR THROAT!

Before taking all of your gold, she looks down upon your slowly draining corpse and smiles wickedly.

Woman: "A Lannister always pays his debts."

Turns out you choose the one woman who had a score to settle with your family a long time ago.
